The Best Movies According to Brandon

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Jerk

Ridiculously funny. Steve Martin is a riot in this film as he portrays a complete idiot that finds fortune in just dumb luck.

Troy

Although inaccurate compared to the writings of Homer, this film is action-packed and never gets old.

Die Hard

Action. Humor. Bruce Willis' bad vacation. I mean...come on! It's Die Hard!
